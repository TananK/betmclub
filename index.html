About Burnaby Entrepreneur Toastmasters

Who We Are

We are a vibrant community for entrepreneurs in Burnaby, honing public speaking and leadership. Our club offers a supportive space to practice, receive feedback, and boost confidence.

Meeting Structure

Our meeting's agenda is as follows:

  • Two to three prepared speeches
  • Table Topics (Guests are welcomed to participate)
  • Evaluations for all speeches and roles
